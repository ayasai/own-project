About sport

  • The anime tells about a unique Japanese program for cultivating the best football striker, which involves many young football players who are eager for success.   • The basketball club of Teiko High School is famous for a great team that has won everything possible in its category. But the five young talents went to five high schools, and only a few knew that besides the famous five, there was also a sixth player who was not much inferior to them.

  • "Volleyball" is an anime about a guy from high school, Shoyo Hinata, who after watching several volleyball games dreams of becoming a professional volleyball player. Hinata's height is only 162 cm, but despite this, the hero still surprises with his abilities. A sports anime about determination, a dream, a desire to be better and never give up.

  • Minato Kiyomizu fell into a coma for 203 days as a result of a car accident. When he wakes up, he realizes that he doesn't remember anything about his years at school, but the memory of past achievements in water polo remains. Then he decides to forget about sports and just return to normal life. But later, he still joins the school's water polo club.
